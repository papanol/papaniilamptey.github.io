Transition to Data Analysis
I started my journey by earning a bachelor's degree, in Mechanical Engineering from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Ghana. It was during my final year's project that I got introduced to the realm of data analytics. At Shamma Beach Resort in Ghana, I worked on a project focused on energy audit and conservation where I gathered and analyzed data related to energy consumption. The knowledge and insights gained from this experience had a lasting impact on me guiding me towards the path of becoming a data analyst.

Academic Achievements
To enhance my abilities and expertise I decided to undertake the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. Additionally, I successfully completed the Data Analytics, for Business Decision-Making program at Durham College. These educational pursuits provided me with a basis, in data analytics and the necessary tools associated with it.
